FAQ

the standard go-to questions

guests

+

  • what do i need to do to have a guest stay with me?
  • give your roommate(s) notice! (you probably set a specific timeframe in your living agreement)
  • can i have a guest stay overnight?
  • yes, as long as your roommate(s) are fine with it
  • what's a guest pass?
  • a guest pass lets your guest get into the building without you signing them in
  • how do i get my guest a guest pass?
  • the form is at the resource center. you and your roommates need to fill it out and then return it to the RC
  • how long can a guest stay?
  • no more than 4 nights in a row, and no more than 6 nights per calendar month
  • do guests have to follow any special rules?
  • they're expected to follow all of the same residential policies you do
